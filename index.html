About Me

Call me Maria. Typical name, but not a typical person. I am a fast-talking, fun-loving, and adventure-seeking IT professional with a growing passion for web design.

I seek to translate my boundless energy into dynamic-yet-functional contents that make browsing in websites a pleasurable experience. Just like how going to theme parks, driving fast (without breaking the speed limit), partying at the beach, and shopping sprees can make people, especially me *wink-wink*, happy.

So I hope you enjoy exploring this new-born cyber crib. Feel free to drop some comments or suggestions; I will appreciate and use them as inspiration.

Thank you.
